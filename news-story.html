Who We Are!

ChiChi's Bakery was first became a topic between two sisters during their usual dinner-time brainstorming session. The first sister, Brooklyn, was an aspiring Graphic Designer, and the second, Cheyenne (ChiChi to her family) was as good a baker as any. The two sisters decided that if they were to open a bakery, Brooklyn would take all of the media operations, and Cheyenne would of course run the bakery and do the actual baking. This worked out great for the two sisters, who were eager to make their mark on the world. The bakery first opened in 2000 in Brooklyn, New York (also part of the plan), and has been thriving ever since. ChiChi's bakery offers up an assortment of goods, from donuts all the way to wedding cakes. We specialize in all desserts pastel, and offer a great selection of breads, coffee, and delicious treats.
